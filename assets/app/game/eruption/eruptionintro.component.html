- ERUPTION -

The Lava Is Coming

A fast paced strategy game

Eruption is a tense, frantic, puzzle game in which you must save as many cities as possible from streams of approaching lava

To protect the cities you must build trenches to divert the lava

There are 2 game modes: 'Speed' and 'Strategy'

Speed and Strategy modes provide two different challenges, race against the lava or plan ahead with limited resources
